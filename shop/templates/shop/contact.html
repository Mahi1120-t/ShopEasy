Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our products and services

What are your shipping options?

We offer standard shipping (3-5 business days), express shipping (2-3 business days), and overnight shipping. Shipping costs vary based on your location and the option selected.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unused items in their original packaging. Returns are free for Indian customers. International returns may be subject to shipping fees.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can also track your order by logging into your account on our website.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary based on the destination. Customs fees may apply for international orders.
